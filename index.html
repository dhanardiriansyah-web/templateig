PEMBAHASAN
Soal: $$ \left (1-\dfrac{1}{2} \right )\left (1-\dfrac{1}{3} \right ) \cdots \left (1-\dfrac{1}{2016} \right ) $$
Langkah 1: Ubah ke Pecahan $$ \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{3}{4} \cdot \frac{4}{5} \cdots \frac{2015}{2016} $$
Langkah 2: Coret Silang (Teleskopik) $$ \frac{1}{\color{red}\bcancel{2}} \cdot \frac{\color{red}\bcancel{2}}{\color{blue}\bcancel{3}} \cdot \frac{\color{blue}\bcancel{3}}{\color{green}\bcancel{4}} \cdots \frac{\color{orange}\bcancel{2015}}{2016} $$
Sisa Angka Awal & Akhir:
$$ \frac{1}{2016} $$
Jawaban: (D)
